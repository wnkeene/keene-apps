EPIC

NASA Image Viewer for iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Vision Pro

About

EPIC brings NASA's vast image archives to your fingertips. Browse the Astronomy Picture of the Day, explore Mars rover photography, view Earth from space through NASA's EPIC camera, stream NASA TV, and search the entire NASA Image Library — all from a single, beautifully designed universal app.

Features

  • Picture of the Day — View today's featured image or browse by date, plus discover random selections from NASA's archive
  • Explore — Browse curated categories including Mars rovers, Earth from space, galaxies, nebulae, the Solar System, the ISS, and more
  • NASA TV — Stream live NASA broadcasts and events directly in the app
  • Search — Search the full NASA Image Library for any topic — galaxies, missions, astronauts, and beyond
  • Favorites — Save your favorite images to a personal library
  • Zoomable Viewer — Tap any image for a full-screen, zoomable viewing experience
  • Downloads — Save high-resolution images to your Mac Downloads folder or iOS Files app
  • Universal App — Works on iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Vision Pro

Platform Support

  • iPhone — iOS 18.0+
  • iPad — iPadOS 18.0+
  • Mac — macOS 15.0+
  • Apple Vision Pro — visionOS 2.0+
