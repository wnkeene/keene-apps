Privacy Policy

HiLoCard - Effective Date: February 18, 2026

Simple Summary: HiLoCard does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal information. Everything stays on your device.

Information Collection and Use

HiLoCard is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal information from users of the app.

Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only using Apple's standard UserDefaults:

  • Your game settings (sound effects, jokers enabled/disabled)
  • Game statistics (wins, losses, current game state)
  • User preferences (e.g., first launch flag)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

No Analytics or Tracking

HiLoCard does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Crash reporting tools
  • Advertising networks
  • Tracking technologies
  • Third-party SDKs that collect data

App Store Review Requests

The app may prompt you to leave a review on the App Store using Apple's standard SKStoreReviewController. This is handled entirely by Apple's systems and does not send any data to us.

External Links

The app may include links to this support website. When you click these links, you will leave the app and visit our website. Standard web server logs may record your IP address and browser type, which are automatically deleted after a reasonable period.

No Account Required

HiLoCard does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personal information to use the app.

Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any information, our app is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

Data Security

Since no data is transmitted from your device, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information through our app.

iCloud Sync

If you purchase HiLoCard, your purchase is managed by Apple and synchronized across your devices via your Apple ID. We do not have access to your Apple ID or purchase information.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: Your game data and preferences are stored locally and never shared
  • Deletion: Simply delete the app to remove all local data
  • Control: You can reset game statistics and change settings anytime within the app

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new policy on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

California Privacy Rights

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected. For HiLoCard: No personal information is collected, sold, or shared.

GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union, HiLoCard complies with GDPR because we do not process any personal data.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit our support page for contact information.
