HiLoCard

Universal Classic War Card Game for iPhone, iPad, and Mac

About the App

HiLoCard is a universal iOS app that brings the classic War card game to all your Apple devices - iPhone, iPad, and Mac. One purchase, three platforms. Featuring beautiful graphics, smooth gameplay, and elegant design optimized for each device. Perfect for a relaxing game session or a quick break from work.

Features

  • Universal App - One app for iPhone, iPad, and Mac (via Mac Catalyst)
  • Two-Player Mode - NEW! Pass & play head-to-head on iPhone and iPad
  • Optimized for Every Screen - Adapts perfectly to iPhone, iPad, and Mac displays
  • Single Player Mode - Challenge the house
  • Split-Screen Design - Intuitive two-player interface (iOS only)
  • Customizable Settings - Toggle jokers and sound effects
  • Beautiful Card Graphics - High-quality vector designs for all Retina displays
  • Simple Gameplay - Easy-to-learn rules with intuitive controls
  • Score Tracking - Monitor your progress throughout each game
  • Offline Play - No internet connection required
  • Native Performance - Built with SwiftUI for smooth, responsive gameplay

Platform Support

  • iPhone - iOS 18.0+ (Single & Two-Player modes)
  • iPad - iOS 18.0+ (Single & Two-Player modes, optimized for all iPad sizes)
  • Mac - macOS 15.0+ (Single Player mode via Mac Catalyst)

One purchase unlocks HiLoCard on all your devices via iCloud.

How to Play

Single Player

You and the house are each dealt a card. The higher card wins the round and scores a point. Play continues until all cards are dealt. Simple, fun, and satisfying!

Two-Player Mode (iPhone & iPad)

Challenge a friend face-to-face! The screen splits to show each player's perspective. Take turns dealing cards and compete to see who scores the most points. Perfect for game nights on your iPhone or iPad!
