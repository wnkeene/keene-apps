Privacy Policy

DotDay

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Simple Summary: DotDay does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal information. Calendar and contact data is accessed through Apple's frameworks and stays on your device and in your iCloud account.

Information Collection and Use

DotDay is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal information from users of the app. DotDay is a free app that does not sell your data and does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

Calendar Access

DotDay uses Apple's EventKit framework to read and create events in calendars you already use. This requires your explicit permission, which you can grant or revoke at any time in your device's Settings.

Calendar data lives in Apple's Calendar and iCloud systems according to your Apple ID settings — not on servers we operate.

Contacts Access

If you use "Choose from Contacts" when creating an event, DotDay presents Apple's system contact picker. You choose a contact in that picker; DotDay uses the name for the event title and an address for the place name if one is available. DotDay does not maintain its own contacts database and does not store or transmit contact data.

Reminders Access

DotDay uses Apple's EventKit framework to read and write reminders in lists you already use. This requires your explicit permission. Reminder data lives in Apple's Reminders and iCloud systems — not on servers we operate.

Data Storage

The app stores the following preference data:

  • Your preferred default calendar selection (stored locally on each device)
  • Your preferred default reminder list and auto-advance setting (stored locally on Mac; synced across iPhone and iPad via Apple's iCloud Key-Value Store)
  • Which calendars are visible in DotDay (stored locally on each device)

Preferences synced via iCloud Key-Value Store are part of Apple's iCloud infrastructure and subject to Apple's iCloud privacy policy. This data is never accessible to us or any third parties outside of Apple's systems.

No Analytics or Tracking

DotDay does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Crash reporting tools
  • Advertising networks
  • Tracking technologies
  • Third-party SDKs that collect data

No Account Required

DotDay does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personal information to use the app.

Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any information, our app is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

Data Security

Since no data is transmitted from your device to our servers, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information through our app.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: Your preferences are stored locally and never shared
  • Deletion: Simply delete the app to remove all local data
  • Permissions: Revoke calendar or reminders access anytime in Settings
  • Control: You can change preferences anytime within the app

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new policy on this page with an updated effective date.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected. For DotDay: No personal information is collected, sold, or shared.

GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union, DotDay complies with GDPR because we do not process any personal data.
