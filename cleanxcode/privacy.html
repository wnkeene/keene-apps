Privacy Policy

CleanXcode

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Simple Summary: CleanXcode does not collect, store, transmit, or share your personal information. It operates locally on your Mac to scan and clean Xcode-related folders you grant access to.

Overview

CleanXcode is a private developer utility for macOS. It is not offered for sale on the Mac App Store. The app is designed with privacy as a core principle: it operates on your local device and does not collect personal data for analytics or marketing.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data from the app for analytics or advertising. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected through the app
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No crash reports sent to developers
  • No network connections for telemetry, marketing, or remote configuration

Local Data

CleanXcode stores the following data locally on your Mac:

  • User preferences (cleaning options, reminder settings)
  • Folder access bookmarks (for permission persistence)
  • Timestamps of last cleaning operations
  • Trash contents (deleted files awaiting permanent deletion or restoration)

This data never leaves your device and is stored using macOS standard preferences and local file storage.

File Access

The app requests access to your Xcode-related folders to scan and clean files. This access is:

  • Granted explicitly by you via macOS permission prompts
  • Used only for scanning and cleaning operations
  • Revocable at any time via System Settings

Third-Party Services

CleanXcode does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
