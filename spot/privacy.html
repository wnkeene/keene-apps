Privacy Policy

Spot Spray Calculator

Summary: Spot Spray Calculator does not collect your personal information. Preferences and recipes stay on your device; recipes may sync via iCloud to your other devices (same Apple ID).

Information Collection and Use

Spot Spray Calculator is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal information from users of the app.

Data Storage

The app stores the following on your device (recipes may sync via iCloud to your other devices):

  • Unit preference (Imperial/Metric)
  • Freemium usage counts
  • Saved spray recipes

Not sent to the developer. iCloud sync is between your devices only.

In-App Purchases

Optional one-time unlock for unlimited use. We do not receive or store payment information.

No Analytics or Tracking

Spot Spray Calculator does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Crash reporting tools
  • Advertising networks
  • Tracking technologies
  • Third-party SDKs that collect data

No Account Required

Spot Spray Calculator does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personal information to use the app.

Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any information, our app is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

Data Security

Since no data is transmitted from your device, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information through our app.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: Your preferences and recipes are stored locally and never shared
  • Deletion: Simply delete the app to remove all local data
  • Control: You can change settings anytime within the app

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new policy on this page.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected. For Spot Spray Calculator: No personal information is collected, sold, or shared.

GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union, Spot Spray Calculator complies with GDPR because we do not process any personal data.
