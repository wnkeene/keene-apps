Privacy Policy

Spot Spray Tank Mix Calculator

Last updated: February 18, 2026

Simple Summary: Spot Calculator does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal information. Everything stays on your device.

Information Collection and Use

Spot Calculator is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal information from users of the app.

Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only using Apple's standard UserDefaults:

  • Your preferred unit system (Imperial or Metric)
  • Saved spray recipes (synced via iCloud if enabled)
  • First launch flag (to show instructions once)

This data never leaves your device (except for iCloud sync to your other devices) and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

No Analytics or Tracking

Spot Calculator does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Crash reporting tools
  • Advertising networks
  • Tracking technologies
  • Third-party SDKs that collect data

No Account Required

Spot Calculator does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personal information to use the app.

Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any information, our app is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

Data Security

Since no data is transmitted from your device, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information through our app.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: Your preferences and recipes are stored locally and never shared
  • Deletion: Simply delete the app to remove all local data
  • Control: You can change settings anytime within the app

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new policy on this page with an updated effective date.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected. For Spot Calculator: No personal information is collected, sold, or shared.

GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union, Spot Calculator complies with GDPR because we do not process any personal data.
