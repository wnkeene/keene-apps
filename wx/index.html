WX

Personal Weather for iPhone, iPad, and Mac

This app is for private use only. WX is not available on the App Store and is not intended for public distribution.

About the App

WX is a personal weather app built for everyday use across iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Save multiple cities, follow your current location with live GPS, and see conditions at a glance with a clean, card-based layout. Forecast data comes from Open-Meteo; weather alerts use official U.S. and Canadian government sources where available.

Features

  • Current Conditions — Temperature, feels-like, humidity, UV index, visibility, pressure, and moon phase
  • Hourly & Daily Forecasts — Scrollable outlooks with sunrise, sunset, and precipitation chances
  • Wind Compass — Live wind direction, speed, and gusts on a native compass view
  • Air Quality — AQI and pollutant readings when available for your location
  • Animated Radar — Precipitation radar with optional hurricane and weather-front overlays
  • Severe Weather Alerts — NWS and ECCC alerts for saved U.S. and Canadian locations
  • Customizable Cards — Show, hide, and reorder weather cards to match how you read the forecast
  • iCloud Sync — Saved cities and settings sync across your devices via iCloud Key-Value storage
  • Background Refresh — Keeps forecasts current when the app is not open

Platform Support

  • iPhone — iOS 18.0+
  • iPad — iPadOS 18.0+
  • Mac — macOS 15.0+
