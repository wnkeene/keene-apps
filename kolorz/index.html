Kolorz

Native Mac color picker — pick from anywhere, copy HEX, RGB, and HSL, with a global shortcut and recent color history.

About

Kolorz is the simplest way to pick a color on macOS and copy it in the format you actually need. It uses Apple's built-in color picker — the same one you already know from Pages, Keynote, and Preview — and adds the things it has always been missing: every color value ready to copy, a global eyedropper shortcut, recent color history, and launch at login. Kolorz is pure SwiftUI and AppKit, runs fully sandboxed on Mac, and does not collect any data. Your colors never leave your Mac.

Quick Start

  1. Open — Launch Kolorz from the Dock, or press ⌥⌘C from any app.
  2. Pick — Press ⌥⌘C or click Pick Color, sample a pixel, and the color panel opens with your pick (or use the system picker above).
  3. Copy — Tap the color square for HEX, or use the copy button on any row.
  4. Reuse — Click a Recent swatch to recall it, or drag colors into Swatches or other apps.

The global shortcut works whenever Kolorz is running. Enable Launch at login in Settings to keep it available.

Features

  • Output Formats — Copy as HEX (#FF6B35), RGB (rgb(255, 107, 53)), or HSL (hsl(16, 100%, 60%))
  • Menus & Shortcuts — Open the color panel from the Dock. Use the Color menu for pick, copy, recent colors, and swatches; Kolorz menu for Settings. ⌥⌘C works globally from any app while Kolorz is running.
  • Pick from Anywhere — Press ⌥⌘C from any app to launch the eyedropper (customizable in Settings); click any pixel on screen with the system magnifier loupe; the color panel opens with your pick. Use the color wheel, sliders, palettes, and image samplers in the panel.
  • Recent Colors — Every sampled color is saved to a persistent history of your last 24 colors; click any swatch to recall it, or drag recent colors into the color panel's swatch grid
  • Copy — Tap the color preview for HEX, click the copy icon on any row, or drag colors into Sketch, Figma, Xcode, or any app that accepts color drops
  • Paste to Convert — Paste any color value into any row and press Return to parse it, apply it to the picker, and update every other format; accepts legacy and modern CSS syntax for HEX, RGB, and HSL with round-trip accuracy
  • Settings — Keep the color panel floating above every other app, start Kolorz at login, and remap the global eyedropper shortcut to any key combination you prefer
  • Keyboard Shortcuts — ⌥⌘C Pick Color (global, customizable), ⌘1 Copy HEX, ⌘2 Copy RGB, ⌘3 Copy HSL
  • Delete Swatches — Choose Color › Delete Swatches… or click Delete in the panel to clear all saved swatches. To remove one swatch, drag it into the trash.
