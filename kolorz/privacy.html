Privacy Policy

Kolorz

Overview

Kolorz is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app operates entirely on your local device and does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No crash reports sent to developers
  • No network connections

Local Data

Kolorz stores the following data locally on your Mac:

  • Recent color history (last 24 picked colors)
  • User preferences (floating panel, launch at login, lowercase HEX, global shortcut)
  • Window positions for the system color panel
  • The system color panel’s saved swatches grid (managed by macOS)

This data never leaves your device and is stored using macOS standard preferences and local file storage.

Clipboard Access

Kolorz writes to your clipboard only when you explicitly request it — by clicking a copy icon, using a copy keyboard shortcut, or dragging the color preview to another app. Kolorz does not read your clipboard automatically; the only time the app reads clipboard contents is when you paste a value into a format field yourself and press Return.

Screen Access

Kolorz uses Apple’s built-in NSColorPanel for color picking. The system magnifier loupe samples a single pixel from your screen only while you actively click the eyedropper. No screen content is captured, recorded, or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

Kolorz does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
