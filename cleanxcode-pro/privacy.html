Privacy Policy

CleanXcode Pro

Last updated: February 18, 2026

Overview

CleanXcode Pro is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app operates entirely on your local device and does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No crash reports sent to developers
  • No network connections except for App Store purchase validation

Local Data

CleanXcode Pro stores the following data locally on your Mac:

  • User preferences (cleaning options, reminder settings)
  • Folder access bookmarks (for permission persistence)
  • Timestamps of last cleaning operations
  • Trash contents (deleted files awaiting permanent deletion or restoration)

This data never leaves your device and is stored using macOS standard preferences and local file storage.

File Access

The app requests access to your Xcode-related folders (Library/Developer and Library/Caches) to scan and clean files. This access is:

  • Granted explicitly by you via macOS permission prompts
  • Used only for scanning and cleaning operations
  • Revocable at any time via System Settings

In-App Purchases

Optional donations are processed entirely by Apple's App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Third-Party Services

CleanXcode Pro does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
